Team

Geodyssey Labs was formed by two principal co-foundes, Luke Skinner and Sashca Scheid. Together they comprise more than 50 years in experience in both geodetic surveying and software production.


Luke Skinner, Chief Executive Officer


Luke is a seasoned professional with more than a decade of experience in software technology. A former product manager and product owner in the field of geodetic surveying, Luke posseses a unique blend of experience in both the field and early stage startups. Luke hepled to develop the Benelux market for products in industrial metrology, and served as Sofware Testing Team Lead in the construction tunneling industry. Additionionally, Luke build outbound sales programs and spear-headed zero-to-one business development efforts for Y-Combinator startups based in Silicon Valley. Luke holds a Bachelor's degree in agriculture and business from Texas A&M University.


Sascha Schneid, Chief Product Officer


Sascha earned his doctorate in the field of geodesy. A respected academic, he is a seasoned professional with over 35 years of experience in the fields of geodetic surveying and geodesy. Formerly a product manager for an industry leading geodesy product, Sascha has authored algorithms which power geodetic surveying jobsites globally today. In addition to his professional roles, Dr. Schneid serves as an Adjunct Lecturer at the University of Applied Sciences in Karlsruhe, Germany. He holds a PhD in Geodesy, emphasizing his academic commitment to the field.
